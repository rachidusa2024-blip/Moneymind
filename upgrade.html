Common questions
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes — absolutely. You can cancel Pro at any time from your account settings. No penalty, no question, no friction. Your data stays available until the end of your billing period.
What happens to my data if I cancel? +
Your data stays in your account. You just return to the Free plan limits. Mina still remembers you and your history — you just have fewer sessions per day.
Is the Lifetime plan really one time? +
Yes. Pay $29 once and Sum Goals is yours forever — including all future features we build. No subscriptions, no renewals, no surprises.
Do you sell my financial data? +
Never. We do not sell your data to anyone — not advertisers, not banks, not data brokers. Your financial information is private and encrypted. No ads. Ever.
Does Mina connect to my bank account? +
No. Mina only knows what you tell her. We cannot and do not access your bank accounts, transactions, or any financial institution. Everything comes from your onboarding conversation.
